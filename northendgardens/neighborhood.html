Steubenville location

Close to parks, downtown destinations, and everyday essentials in Steubenville.

Northend Gardens Apartments is located at 730 N 7th St, Steubenville, OH 43952. From here, residents are near North End Park, the downtown riverfront area, Fort Steuben Park, and multiple local transit stops that make getting around town easier.

Nearby highlights
  • North End Park is about 0.2 miles away for green space and outdoor time.
  • The Flats riverfront area is within a short walk for an open neighborhood feel.
  • Fort Steuben Park and the Steubenville Marina are roughly 0.8 miles to 1 mile away.
  • Franciscan University transit stops and downtown bus connections are nearby.
